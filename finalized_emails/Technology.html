Compared to previous approaches, Microsoft's Topological Core offers a significant advantage in scalability and controllability.

The ability to manipulate and measure Majorana particles digitally simplifies the quantum computing process and opens the door to building larger, more powerful quantum systems. With Majorana 1, Microsoft is taking a giant leap towards a future where quantum computers can revolutionize various industries and scientific disciplines.
